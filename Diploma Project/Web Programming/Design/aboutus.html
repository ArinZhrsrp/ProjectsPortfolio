ABOUT US

The online shopping we aim to develop is a system that approve company and personal shop separately and user-friendly. The interface for seller and customer will be different.

For seller which use the name of their company need to show their documentary to open a online shop. By contrast, reseller is needed to show their supplier information and their own documentary. The supplier's documentary will be send to the related company to be confirm.

Registration of user will ask for their personal information such as nickname, name on IC, home address, phone number, and etc. Some of the information will only be used for system processing and it is not visible for others on their profile page.

Seller and reseller should done registration for the normal user first then will be ask for soft copy documentary with the marking for the "For xxx use only" to carry out their marketing activities or sale.

All use of the system should agree the policies first to be continue. For people under 15years old will be ask for their parent together with them while registration.
